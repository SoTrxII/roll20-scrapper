Convert Tokens and Pages to Updated Dynamic Lighting

Convert Lighting will add the Updated Dynamic Lighting settings for the Pages you select, including the tokens placed on those pages. Optionally, Convert Lighting can change the Tokens in the game’s Journal.

Use Caution! This tool makes changes to your data.

Protect your game data by making a backup copy. You can also choose to only convert individual pages to try it out.

Going back to the legacy system is as simple as changing the Page Settings.

Curious what changes the tool is making? Find out in the Roll20 Help Center.
